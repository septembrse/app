
# How do I contact the SeptembRSE Conference Committee?

You can contact the committee via a number of different channels;

1. [Email us](mailto:conference-2021@society-rse.org) at conference-2021@society-rse.org.
2. [Post an anonymous message](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfKgxH21k4yCrVKqBtKsG0vceKU0v4hA8U6JV2AHQ3jWwtMtw/viewform?usp=sf_link)
3. Fill in our [feedback survey](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSekBGx1-LdtZQJpBtLTBxeO4qukInkWPPCcsHKasKm3PysvpA/viewform?usp=sf_link)
4. Talk to us in the Virtual Conference Center. Committee members will have stars next to their names.

You can also find links to the above in the Virtual Conference Center at the
SeptembRSE stand.

# How do I share my experience of SeptembRSE?

Please feel free to share your experience of SeptembRSE. We recommend
sharing using the [#septembrse](https://twitter.com/search?q=%23septembrse) hashtag, e.g. on Twitter.

There is also a #septembrse channel in the RSE Slack space.

# Code of conduct

Please remember our [code of conduct](https://septembrse.society-rse.org/policies/code-of-conduct/)
and be respectful and considerate in all of your communications.
